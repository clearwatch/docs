# Monitoring Pages

By default, Clearwatch monitors your homepage. There are two ways to monitor additional pages:

## Option 1: Add pages manually

From your [account page](https://clearwatch.app/manage), click **"+ Add a page to monitor"** on any site. Enter a path like `/checkout`, `/contact`, or `/products` and we'll start monitoring it.

No verification needed. You can add any page you want to monitor.

## Option 2: Automatic page discovery

If you [verify ownership](/verification/overview) of your site, Clearwatch reads your sitemap and automatically discovers important pages to monitor. This is useful if you have many pages and don't want to add them one by one.

## Page limits

The number of pages we monitor depends on your plan:

| Plan | Max pages |
|------|-----------|
| Free | 10 |
| Starter | 100 |
| Pro | 1,000 |

This includes both manually added pages and automatically discovered pages.

## Why monitor more than your homepage?

Your homepage could be fine while your checkout page is broken. Monitoring key pages catches issues that homepage-only monitoring would miss.
