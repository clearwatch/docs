# Page Discovery

When you verify ownership of your site, Clearwatch reads your sitemap and discovers important pages to monitor.

## Why it matters

Instead of just checking your homepage, we'll monitor your key pages — product pages, contact forms, checkout flows — and alert you if any of them go down.

Your homepage could be fine while your checkout page is broken. Page discovery catches that.

## Page limits

The number of pages we monitor depends on your plan:

| Plan | Max pages |
|------|-----------|
| Free | 10 |
| Starter | 100 |
| Pro | 1,000 |

## How to enable it

Page discovery requires [site verification](/verification/overview). Once your site is verified, we'll automatically discover pages from your sitemap.

## What if I don't verify?

Without verification, we only monitor your homepage. You'll still get downtime alerts and weekly reports — just for the one page.
