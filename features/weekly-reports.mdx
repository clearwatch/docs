# Weekly Reports

Every week, you'll receive a summary for each of your monitored sites.

## What's included

- **Uptime percentage** — How much of the week your site was up
- **Average response time** — How fast your site responded
- **Total checks** — How many times we checked your site
- **Incidents** — Any downtime events that occurred

If there were no incidents during the week, we'll let you know everything was fine.

## Page discovery upsell

If you're on the free plan and we've discovered additional pages on your site, the report will let you know how many pages you could be monitoring with an upgrade.
