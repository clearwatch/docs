# Status Pages

Each monitored site gets a public status page showing 90 days of uptime history. Available on Starter and Pro plans.

## What's shown

- Current status (up, down, or slow)
- 90-day uptime history
- Response time data
- Recent incidents with duration

## Your status page URL

Your status page is available at:

```
clearwatch.app/status/your-domain.com
```

Share this with customers, stakeholders, or anyone who wants to see your site's uptime.
