# Account Management

Manage your account at [clearwatch.app/manage](https://clearwatch.app/manage).

## Signing in

1. Enter your email address
2. We'll send you a 6-digit code
3. Enter the code to access your account

Your session lasts 7 days. After that, you'll need to sign in again with a new code.

## What you can do

From your account page you can:

- **View your monitored sites** — See all the sites Clearwatch is monitoring for you
- **Remove a site** — Stop monitoring a site you no longer need
- **Verify site ownership** — Add a meta tag to enable page discovery
- **Manage your subscription** — Upgrade, downgrade, or cancel via Stripe

## Accessing your account

You can reach your account page from:

- The **Account** link in the website footer
- The **Manage your sites** link in your weekly report email
- Directly at [clearwatch.app/manage](https://clearwatch.app/manage)
