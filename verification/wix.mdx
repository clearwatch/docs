# Verify on Wix

## Step 1: Copy your meta tag

Find your verification meta tag on your [account page](https://clearwatch.app/manage).

## Step 2: Open Custom Code settings

1. Go to your Wix dashboard
2. Navigate to **Settings → Custom Code**
3. Click **Add Code**

## Step 3: Add the meta tag

1. Paste your meta tag in the code field
2. Set **Placement** to **Head**
3. Set **Apply to** to **All Pages**
4. Click **Apply**
5. **Publish** your site

## Step 4: Verify

Go back to your [Clearwatch account page](https://clearwatch.app/manage) and click **Verify** on your site.
