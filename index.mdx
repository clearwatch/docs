# Welcome to Clearwatch

Clearwatch monitors your website in the background and emails you if something needs attention. No dashboards, no noise — just alerts when something matters.

## What you get

- **24/7 uptime monitoring** — We watch your site around the clock and email you the moment something breaks
- **Weekly SRE brief** — A plain-language report every Monday covering uptime, performance, and anything that needs your attention
- **Performance insights** — We test your homepage speed and tell you what's slowing it down, in words you can act on
- **SSL certificate monitoring** — We alert you 7 days before your SSL expires, so visitors never see a security warning
- **Page discovery** — We find and monitor important pages on [verified sites](/verification/overview), not just your homepage
- **Public status pages** — Share a live status page with 90 days of uptime history with your customers

## How it works

1. Enter your website URL and email on [clearwatch.app](https://clearwatch.app)
2. Verify your email by clicking the link we send you
3. We start monitoring your site immediately

That's it. No servers to configure, no code to install, no dashboards to check.
