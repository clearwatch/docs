# Welcome to Clearwatch

Clearwatch monitors your website in the background and emails you if something needs attention. No dashboards, no noise — just alerts when something matters.

## What you get

- **Uptime monitoring** — We check your site every few minutes and email you if it goes down
- **Weekly reports** — A summary of your site's uptime and performance delivered to your inbox every Monday
- **SSL monitoring** — We alert you if your SSL certificate is about to expire
- **Status pages** — A public page showing 90 days of uptime history
- **Page discovery** — We find and monitor important pages on [verified sites](/verification/overview) automatically

## How it works

1. Enter your website URL and email on [clearwatch.app](https://clearwatch.app)
2. Verify your email by clicking the link we send you
3. We start monitoring your site immediately

That's it. No servers to configure, no code to install, no dashboards to check.
